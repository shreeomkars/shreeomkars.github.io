Introduction

  • Shree Omkar Steel Trading llc is a stockholder and distributor of structural steel products, pipes, wire mesh, wire mesh products, galvanized steel and GI steel products in the UAE, catering to over 2000 clients. We are one of the most competitive and experienced names of steel in the UAE. With operations from warehouses in DIP and Musaffah Abu dhabi. Shree Omkar Steel is not just a supplier of steel but a valued partner in offering a multitude of services.
